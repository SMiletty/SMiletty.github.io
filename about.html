Our Story

The Rusty Plough started with a simple passion: crafting delicious, high-quality burgers that bring families and friends together. We renovated our space from an old barn to create a warm, inviting atmosphere for our customers.

Our Mission

We're committed to serving fresh, flavorful burgers made with premium ingredients and exceptional service. Every burger is prepared with care and passion to ensure your satisfaction.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients
  • Customizable menu options
  • Friendly, helpful staff
  • Cozy, welcoming atmosphere
